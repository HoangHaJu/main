Registration Policies

Please read our Registration & Return Policy carefully before registering for TESOL ELevate. By completing registration for the event, you consent to abide by these policies. Any questions about policies should be addressed to TESOL Learn.

Payment

Payment can be by credit card, purchase order, and PayPal. TESOL accepts VISA, Master Card, and American Express. 

Refunds

Refunds are not issued after 6 October 2024. You must request a refund in writing, and it must be received by 6 October 2024. Send refund requests via email to learn@tesol.org.

Cancellations

TESOL reserves the right to cancel any events because of low registration or events beyond TESOL's control. In the unlikely event of cancellation, registrants will be notified and offered the option to switch to a different event or receive a full refund.

Code of Conduct

All event attendees must abide by the TESOL code of conduct.
