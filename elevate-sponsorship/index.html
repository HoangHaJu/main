ELevate Sponsorship

Why Sponsor TESOL ELevate 2024?

Sponsorship Benefits

  1. Connect with the international audience of English language teaching professionals that only TESOL International Association can bring you! TESOL ELevate 2024 will present four live 90-minute interactive online workshops, 16 prerecorded 45-minute on-demand sessions, teaching tips from around the world, and several networking opportunities. Themes include: Social & Environmental Responsibility, Technology Integration, Methodologies & Multiliteracies, and Teacher Leadership & Professionalism.
  2. Reach an audience that may be unable to attend to our in-person events.
  3. Showcase your organization within the digital platform in a live dialogue format or through advertising banners or short commercials in between sessions.
  4. Create engaging push notifications to participants.
  5. Be seen as a leaders in the global primary/secondary teacher ELT community.
  6. Sessions can be recorded and shared with your various networks.

Audience

Audience: Primary and secondary educators worldwide

TESOL ELevate 2024 will bring together ELT professionals for a 2-day event that strengthens English language teaching worldwide. Participants will:

  • Walk away with tips, strategies, and resources to use in the classroom right away.
  • Be able to meet, connect, and share ideas with peers from different countries.
  • Gain a deeper understanding of important issues that impact teachers, learners, and the ELT field.
