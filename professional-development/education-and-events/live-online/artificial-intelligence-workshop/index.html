"AI in the English Language Classroom" Workshop

With so much information coming out so quickly about Artificial Intelligence (AI), many teachers have found that they’ve read more about AI than they have spent time exploring it. With the potential to save you time when creating classroom materials and lesson plans and to improve outcomes for your multilingual learners of English, now is the time to get started with AI. The “AI in the English Language Classroom” workshop is designed to help kickstart your journey into using tools like ChatGPT, Bing, Bard, Midjourney, or any of the myriad tools that seem to be popping up every day. After learning more general strategies for using AI tools in the classroom, you’ll have ample time to test out the tools and exchange ideas with your peers about how best to apply them to your teaching context.

Led by an expert in the field, this highly interactive and participant-centered workshop will equip you with a starter kit of knowledge and tools to explore AI in your learning spaces to benefit you and your learners. This live online workshop is 7.5 hours total, taking place 19-23 February 2024 from 6:00 - 7:30 p.m. ET each day.

All sessions will be recorded and available on the TESOL Canvas platform for participants for a month following the workshop.

Once you complete the workshop, you'll receive a digital badge you can share online! An electronic certificate of completion can be provided upon request.

Register by 16 February 2024. It’s going to be a blast, and we’re looking forward to seeing you there!

More About This Workshop

In this workshop, we will explore:

  • What AI is and what it means for language teaching and learning.
  • How to use chatbots like ChatGPT with your learners.
  • Prompt engineering for beginners on up!
  • Ways to innovate with image generators.
  • Techniques to save time at the teacher’s desk.
  • Plagiarism, ethics, and problems with AI.
  • And more!

Participants will be given the most valuable resource – TIME – to explore the tools and discuss their findings with each other. This course is a collaborative effort designed to give everybody a practical and applicable understanding of AI in the language learning classroom.

Who Should Attend?

This workshop is for English language teaching professionals who want to learn more about AI and how to use it in their classrooms.

What Will I Learn?

After completing this workshop, you will be able to:

  • Save time creating classroom materials and lesson plans.
  • Identify and implement the key elements to engineer chatbot prompts.
  • Develop customized AI-based strategies to use with your learners and in your work.
  • Create student-centered AI policies for your classroom.

Questions? Contact us.

About the Instructor

Brent Warner is an award-winning community college professor focusing on integrating technology and innovation into the language learning process. He works with teachers and organizations across the globe to provide practical advice for helping English language learners take advantage of tech to help them communicate more clearly. He blogs about technology integration in the ESOL classroom for TESOL, and he is the co-host of The DIESOL Podcast, focusing on innovation in ESOL. He also recently launched AIinESL.com, a site focused on helping teachers understand how we can use ESL in our classes.
