About the TESOL International Convention & Expo

The convention offers a wide range of engaging professional development opportunities to English language teaching (ELT) professionals at all levels from around the world. In lively interactive sessions, you'll develop a global perspective through the exchange of ideas, research, and practices. Gain knowledge about current trends in the field while developing your professional network.

You'll also have the opportunity to connect with English language teaching-related companies and organizations at the Expo! Learn about the latest products, publications, services, and resources in the field.

If you're interested in reaching leaders in the field, take a look at our prospectus to learn about becoming an exhibitor or sponsor!
