About TESOL Connections

TESOL Connections (TC), a monthly newsletter, is designed exclusively for English language teaching professionals. It features:

  • Practical articles.  
  • Timely resources.  
  • Opinions and editorials.
  • Quizzes and fun activities.
  • TESOL International Association news.

TC articles reflect TESOL’s support for the profession and linguistic and cultural diversity. 

TESOL Connections has been in publication since 2011.   

TESOL members automatically have access to the TESOL Connections website as a member benefit. Members use the same login credentials they use to access the TESOL website. 

If members would like to receive the TESOL Connections email when a new issue is published, they will select TESOL Connections under Communication Preferences in their TESOL account.

Visit our subscription page to learn about all subscription options!
