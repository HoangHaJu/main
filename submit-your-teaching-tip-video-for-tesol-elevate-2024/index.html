Submit a Teaching Tip!

We're excited to announce an open call for teaching tip videos for TESOL ELevate 2024, happening this October! We're searching for engaging, practical, and impactful tips that will inspire and empower TESOL professionals around the world. 

What We're Looking For

  • Short and Sweet: Videos should be no more than 3 minutes long.
  • Actionable Tips: Share a specific teaching strategy or technique that can be easily implemented in the classroom.

Topics

  • Social and environmental responsibility
  • Technology integration
  • Methodologies and multiliteracies
  • Teacher leadership and professionalism

Why Submit a Video?

  • Share Your Expertise: Become a recognized thought leader in the TESOL community.
  • Inspire Others: Help fellow educators improve their teaching practice.
  • Gain Recognition: Get your work showcased at TESOL ELevate 2024!

Submission Deadline: 1 October 2024

Submission

  • Fill out your information and upload your video to a cloud storage service (TESOL Google Drive folder)
  • Fill out the information form:
    • Your name and title
    • Title of your teaching tip
    • Topic of your teaching tip

Additional Tips

  • Keep your video concise and engaging.
  • Use clear visuals and audio.

We can't wait to see your amazing teaching tips!
